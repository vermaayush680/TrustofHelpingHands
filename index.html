About Us

We, at Trust Of Helping Hands believe in women empowerement and equality. We help the poor by providing them with basic necessities as well as the skills that can help them earn a living.

Help us leave this world better than we found it

Women Empowerment

Empowering women is one of the main aims of our trust. Each woman of our society deserves respect and equality and all the opportunities in this corporate world.

Covid Crisis

During the covid crisis daily wage workers had to walk hundreds of miles barefoot without any shelter or basic necessities. We helped many such people by providing them food and shelter.

Saving the environment

We have been planting trees consistently.We believe that global warming is a threat to this world and that with constant efforts we can overcome it.

Helping the kids

We pay for the education of many kids below the poverty line and with no means of education. We believe that education is everyone's right and no one should be deprived of it.
